SmartEnergy AI

An AI-powered energy forecasting and carbon footprint advisor — built for SMEs and campuses across India, and submitted as the capstone for the 1M1B AI for Sustainability internship.

SDG 7 · Affordable & Clean Energy — SDG 13 · Climate Action

Turning raw consumption data into action

SmartEnergy AI reads in energy usage patterns and turns them into forecasts and plain-language carbon advice. Underneath it pairs IBM's Granite LLM with a retrieval-augmented pipeline and agentic workflows, so the system doesn't just predict — it reasons over context and recommends what to change.
